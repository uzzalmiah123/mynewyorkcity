    About New York City

    New York City is a larger city in the USA. It is 302.6 square miles long, and it has five boroughs which are Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island. It also has a Statue of Liberty and tallest building USA which is One World Trade Center. Each of boroughs is part of NY county, With an estimated 2017 population of 8.623 million.
